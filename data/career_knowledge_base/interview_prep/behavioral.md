# Behavioral Interview Preparation

## Common Interview Formats

### STAR Method Framework
**Situation:**
- Set the context
- Be specific about when/where
- Keep it concise

**Task:**
- What was your responsibility?
- What needed to be accomplished?
- Define the challenge

**Action:**
- What specific steps did YOU take?
- Use "I" not "we"
- Highlight your contribution

**Result:**
- Quantify outcomes
- Lessons learned
- Impact on business/organization

## Core Competencies

### Leadership
**Questions:**
- "Tell me about a time you led a project"
- "Describe a difficult team member situation"
- "How do you motivate others?"

**Preparation Points:**
- Delegation examples
- Conflict resolution
- Mentoring experiences
- Decision-making process

### Teamwork & Collaboration
**Questions:**
- "Describe a successful team project"
- "How do you handle disagreements?"
- "Tell me about cross-functional work"

**Preparation Points:**
- Communication examples
- Compromise situations
- Remote collaboration
- Feedback giving/receiving

### Problem Solving
**Questions:**
- "Describe a complex problem you solved"
- "Tell me about a failed project"
- "How do you approach ambiguity?"

**Preparation Points:**
- Analytical process
- Creative solutions
- Risk assessment
- Learning from failure

### Communication
**Questions:**
- "Explain a technical concept to non-technical people"
- "How do you handle difficult stakeholders?"
- "Describe a presentation you gave"

**Preparation Points:**
- Stakeholder management
- Documentation examples
- Presentation skills
- Active listening

## Common Question Categories

### About You
1. "Tell me about yourself"
2. "Why are you interested in this role?"
3. "What are your strengths/weaknesses?"
4. "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

### Work Experience
1. "What was your most challenging project?"
2. "Describe your biggest accomplishment"
3. "Tell me about a time you failed"
4. "How do you prioritize work?"

### Situational
1. "What would you do in [specific scenario]?"
2. "How do you handle tight deadlines?"
3. "Describe working with a difficult colleague"
4. "How do you handle conflicting priorities?"

## Company-Specific Preparation

### Research Areas:
- Company mission and values
- Recent news and products
- Company culture
- Interviewer backgrounds (if known)

### Tailor Your Answers:
- Align with company values
- Use company-specific terminology
- Reference their products/services
- Show industry knowledge

## Preparation Strategy

### Week 1: Self-Assessment
- List 10-15 accomplishments
- Identify key skills and strengths
- Prepare your "Tell me about yourself" pitch
- Map experiences to common questions

### Week 2: Story Development
- Create 5-7 STAR stories
- Practice each story
- Get feedback from peers
- Record and review yourself

### Week 3: Mock Interviews
- Practice with friends/mentors
- Use interview platforms
- Time your responses
- Work on non-verbal communication

## Red Flags to Avoid
- Blaming others for failures
- Speaking negatively about past employers
- Vague or rambling answers
- Lack of self-awareness
- Overly rehearsed responses

## Questions to Ask Interviewers
### About the Role:
- "What does success look like in this position?"
- "What are the biggest challenges facing this team?"
- "How is performance measured?"

### About the Team:
- "What's the team culture like?"
- "How does the team collaborate?"
- "What opportunities are there for mentorship?"

### About Growth:
- "What opportunities exist for professional development?"
- "How does the company support learning?"
- "What career paths have others in this role taken?"
